REMOTE SENSING

Welcome to the official webpage of the Remote Sensing Research Group, Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati. The various focus areas of our group include the followings:

  • Machine Learning
  • Pattern Recognition
  • Remote Sensing
  • Image Processing
  • Agriculture Monitoring
  • Big-data Analysis

The group investigates on the applied domains of machine learning where a primary focus is on designing automatic techniques for land-cover classification by analysing remotely sensed satellite images. We are looking to expand our horizon with new domains and fresh members. If you are interested to be a part of our research, please feel free to get in touch with us here

In modern time, a large volume of remotely sensed images of the earth’s surface is available on a daily basis banking on a large number of satellites generating very-high-resolution images frequently with a low revisit time. This demands the use of modern machine learning-based tools and techniques for better remote sensing for the long-standing goal of real-time earth surface monitoring. This development may revolutionise various fields like disaster mitigation, crop monitoring, deforestation control, soil contamination surveillance, water pollution, bio-geochemical cycling, thermal mapping, international border protection, etc, in the future.
